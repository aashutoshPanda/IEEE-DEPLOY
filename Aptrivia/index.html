  • About : Aptrivia

    Aptrivia is an aptitude/reasoning championship organised across numerous schools of Chhattisgarh. It is spearheaded by the students of IEEE SB, NIT Raipur. It aims to recognise and reward young dexterous minds of of Chhattisgarh, accomplished by putting young, inquisitive and enthusiastic minds through a carefully designed examination which is meant to put their brains to work.

Participation Details and Rules

Participation maybe in a team of two or one. Both participants must belong to the same school. Teams of one get no advantage over teams of two whatsoever. Both participants of the team must belong to same category.



Mode of Examination

The mode of examination is offline. The exam will be conducted on the said date in the registered schools. Registration for the exam is available only in the offline mode.

Categories

The exam will be conducted separately for the following categories :
1. Class 7 and 8
2. Class 9 and 10

Exam Fee

Price of participation shall be Rs.150 per team irrespective of the number of members in the team.
