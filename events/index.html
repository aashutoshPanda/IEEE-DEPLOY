Recent Events

A glimpse into our glorious past.

It was an online promotional event organised to celebrate and enjoy the colours of Holi in an engineer’s style. We all are surrounded by machines and just like Rachodas Chanchad every engineer or technocrat has his/her unique definition of machine. So, to celebrate and reward unique thinking we organised this event in which the participants had to click a photograph with a not so general entity which they think is a machine, immersed in the colours of Holi with a catchy caption describing his/her definition of a machine.

In the month of February, we organised a 2-day workshop in collaboration with Google Crowdsource Community, Mumbai. The speaker for the same was none other than Mr. Ali Mustafa, Point-of-Contact, Indian Pacific, Google. The motive behind this was to provide the participants with an opportunity to undertake projects and find solutions to these critical problems under the guidance of industry experts.


Enigma was a two-day fun event comprising of 2 rounds wiz, Aptitude & Logical Reasoning and the main event. It tested not only the participant’s mental strength but also their physical strength and endurance.

It was a 2-day fun event organised during Aavartan – Annual Techfest, NIT Raipur. The much-awaited movie of 2018 had some elements of surprise and much anticipation.

The motto of your student branch is to not only be an asset for the student members in and around our city but also for the under-privileged. So, to enumerate Diwali and celebrate it in its true colours we undertook a humanitarian activity to provide 10% of the total fund generated through our major events as donation towards this noble cause. Also, our members had a visit to the orphanage bearing toys and sweets. The children thoroughly enjoyed their company and it was just an unforgettable experience for our team as well.

It was a 2-day workshop on Artificial intelligence jointly organised by Techienest Jaipur and Team Techkriti IIT Kanpur. It was a workshop cum selection round for All India Research Championship which is to be held at IIT Kanpur during the month of March. All the teams were provided with kits containing an Arduino Uno, Wi-Fi Module and other components.


It was a two-day workshop on Embedded System in association with Robosapiens India. The workshop started with the introduction of microcontrollers and programming in embedded C. The teams were given kits consisting of an Atmega 8 microcontroller, IR sensors and other components.

Cryptics was an online quiz game organised on the facebook page of IEEE Student Branch NIT Raipur. It was a 15-day event, an online quiz but with twists. Everyday 3 questions were posted at the facebook page at any random time. Participants had to send their reply as comments on the image of the question that was uploaded. But there was a small twist, the topic for the next day questions was to be declared by the person last on the leader board.

Enigma was a two-day fun event comprising of 3 rounds wiz, Aptitude & Logical Reasoning; Quiz and Treasure Hunt. It tested not only the participant’s mental strength but also their physical strength and endurance.
